Thai Peanut Chicken & Veggies (4 servings)
Ingredients
Protein & Veggies

- 1 lb (16 oz) chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces (or firm tofu if swapping)
- 3 cups broccoli florets (~1 large head)
- 2 bell peppers (red or yellow), sliced
- 2 cups snap peas (or snow peas)
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  Peanut Sauce
- ¼ cup natural peanut butter (unsweetened, no added sugar)
- 2 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce (or tamari / coconut aminos)
- Juice of 1 lime
- 2–3 Tbsp water (to thin to desired consistency)
- 1 tsp sesame oil (optional, for flavor)
- Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  Grain Base
- 1 cup dry quinoa (makes ~3 cups cooked)
  Optional Garnish
- Chopped cilantro
- Chopped peanuts

Instructions

1. Cook quinoa: Rinse 1 cup dry quinoa, cook according to package (~15 minutes). Fluff and set aside.
2. Prep sauce: In a bowl, whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, water, sesame oil (if using), garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes until smooth. Adjust thickness with extra water.
3. Cook chicken: Heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in a large skillet/wok over medium-high. Add chicken and cook ~5–6 min until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
4. Cook veggies: In the same skillet, add broccoli, peppers, snap peas, and mushrooms. Stir-fry 4–5 min until crisp-tender.
5. Combine: Return chicken to pan, pour sauce over, and toss until coated and warmed through.
6. Serve: Spoon over quinoa. Garnish with cilantro or chopped peanuts if desired.

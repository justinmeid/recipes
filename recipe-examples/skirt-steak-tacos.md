Flip and Finish
2 minutes
Flip the steak over. Sear the second side for another 2 minutes. Skirt steak tastes best at medium-rare to medium (internal temp of 130°F to 135°F). Because it is so thin, it cooks fast! 3. Rest and Slice (The Make-or-Break Step)
Once it's done, move the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for 5 to 10 minutes before touching it. If you cut it immediately, all the delicious juices will run out onto the board and leave the steak dry.
How to Slice: Look closely at the steak to see which way the long muscle fibers run (the grain). Take your knife and slice perpendicularly (against) the grain into thin strips. If you slice with the grain, it will be tough and chewy; slicing against it breaks up the fibers so it completely melts in your mouth

## Instructions

1. **Season the chicken:** Trim the fat off the thighs if you like. Toss with the soy
   sauce, avocado oil, and 1 grated garlic clove, then sprinkle with the taco seasoning and
   mix.
2. **Cook the chicken:** Air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes until crusty, golden, and juicy
   (165°F internal). Oven: bake at 425°F for about 20 minutes.
3. **Make the sauce:** Blend the avocado, mayo, cotija, cilantro, the remaining garlic
   clove, the juice of 1–2 limes, and 2 tbsp water until smooth. Thin with more water or
   thicken with more avocado/mayo as needed.
4. **Finish:** Let the chicken rest a few minutes, then chop or pull into bite-sized pieces
   and squeeze lime over it. Build tacos with a dollop of sauce, a pile of chicken, and
   diced onion and cilantro.

## Notes

Every taco seasoning is different — Siete (mild) is the source's go-to. A little cornstarch
in the blend helps the chicken crisp up golden.

## Instructions

1. **Cook quinoa:** Rinse 1 cup dry quinoa and cook per package directions (~15 minutes).
   Fluff and set aside.
2. **Prep sauce:** Whisk the peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, 2–3 tbsp water, sesame
   oil, grated garlic and ginger, and red pepper flakes until smooth. Thin with more water
   as needed.
3. **Cook chicken:** Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high. Add the
   chicken and cook 5–6 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
4. **Cook veggies:** In the same skillet, stir-fry the broccoli, peppers, snap peas, and
   mushrooms 4–5 minutes until crisp-tender.
5. **Combine:** Return the chicken, pour over the sauce, and toss until coated and warmed
   through.
6. **Serve:** Spoon over the quinoa and garnish with cilantro or chopped peanuts.

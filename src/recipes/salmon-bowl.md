## Instructions

1. Slice the salmon into large ~1-inch cubes, removing the skin and any bones.
2. Make the marinade: combine the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and sesame oil, then grate
   in the garlic and ginger and mix. Add the salmon and coat. Marinate 10 minutes (or a few
   hours in the fridge).
3. While the salmon marinates, slice the cucumber, shred the carrot, dice the avocado, and
   defrost the edamame under running water.
4. **Pan-fry:** Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high. Cook the salmon cubes in a
   single layer for 5–7 minutes, flipping halfway and pouring over the leftover marinade.
   **Air fryer:** 390°F for 5–8 minutes, flipping halfway.
5. Assemble bowls with the rice, veggies, avocado, and edamame, then top with the salmon and
   an optional drizzle of spicy mayo or extra marinade.

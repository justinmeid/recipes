## Instructions

1. **Marinate:** Blend the garlic, onion, lime juice, orange juice, cumin, oregano, smoked
   paprika, chili powder, cilantro, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a food processor. Pour
   over the steak in a bag and marinate as long as possible.
2. **Heat the skillet (5–7 min):** Set a dry cast-iron skillet over high heat until a drop
   of water dances and evaporates instantly. Add 1 tbsp high-smoke-point oil and swirl; it
   should shimmer and just start to smoke.
3. **First sear (2–3 min):** Lay the steak down (cut in half to fit if needed). Don't move
   it — let a dark brown crust form.
4. **Flip and finish (2 min):** Sear the second side. Skirt steak is best medium-rare to
   medium (130–135°F) and cooks fast because it's thin.
5. **Rest and slice:** Rest 5–10 minutes. Find the grain, then slice thin strips
   perpendicular (against) the grain so it stays tender.

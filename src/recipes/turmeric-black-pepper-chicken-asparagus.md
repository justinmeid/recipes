## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together ¼ cup water with the honey, black pepper, and ½ tsp salt;
   set this honey mixture aside.
2. In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, turmeric, and 1 tsp salt. Add the chicken and
   toss to coat.
3. Heat the oil in a 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add the chicken and cook
   until the turmeric is fragrant and the chicken is golden on both sides, 2–3 minutes per
   side.
4. Add the asparagus, season with salt, stir, and cook until crisp-tender, 1–2 minutes.
5. Add the honey mixture and cook, stirring, until the chicken is cooked through and the
   sauce thickens, 2–3 minutes.
6. Off the heat, stir in the vinegar. Season to taste and serve with a squeeze of lime.

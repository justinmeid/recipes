## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
2. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Rub the
   chicken with the remaining 1 tbsp oil, oregano, paprika, red pepper flakes, salt, and
   pepper. Sear until golden, about 3–5 minutes per side, then remove from the skillet.
3. Add the butter and shallot to the same skillet; cook until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
   Add the garlic and orzo and cook until lightly golden, 2–3 minutes. Pour in the wine to
   deglaze, then add 1½ cups water. Bring to a boil and cook 3–5 minutes.
4. Stir in the cream, mustard, parmesan, spinach, and sun-dried tomatoes until the spinach
   wilts. Slide the chicken and its juices back in. Transfer to the oven and bake, uncovered,
   10–15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
5. Serve topped with lemon juice and rosemary, if desired.

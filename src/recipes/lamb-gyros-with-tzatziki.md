## Instructions

1. **Tzatziki:** Grate the cucumber and stir together with the Greek yogurt, grated garlic,
   juice of ½–1 lemon (to taste), olive oil, and ½ tsp salt. Set aside in the fridge.
2. **Soak the panko:** In a small bowl, combine the panko with the milk and let soak at
   least 10 minutes.
3. **Mix the meatballs:** In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, onion, garlic, mint,
   egg, zest of 1 lemon, oregano, cumin, coriander, black pepper, and 1 tsp salt. Add the
   soaked breadcrumbs and mix until just combined.
4. **Cook the meatballs:** Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium. Working in batches,
   scoop ~2 tbsp dollops onto the pan and flatten slightly. Cook 2–3 minutes per side until
   browned and the internal temp reaches 165°F. Keep warm under foil while you finish the
   rest.
5. **Toast the pitas:** Brush lightly with oil on both sides and grill on a hot pan ~1
   minute per side until warm and toasted.
6. **Assemble:** Spread tzatziki on each hot pita, add 3–4 meatballs, the sliced veggies,
   and more tzatziki on top. Wrap the bottom half in foil or parchment to hold the "U" shape.
